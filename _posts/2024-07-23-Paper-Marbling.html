--- layout: post title: Paper Marbling --- Paper Marbling.
Right click to drop paint onto canvas. The paint drop was from this website.
The combing is done by left clicking and dragging.
The combing calculation is from this paper.
I tweaked a couple calculations, like x = xb, y = (P-B) x N to get rid of unnecessary calculations.
Currently, everything is done on CPU. After making many circles, the latency starts to be noticible.
